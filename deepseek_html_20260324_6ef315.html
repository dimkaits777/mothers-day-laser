10 Mother's Day Embroidery Ideas

March 15, 2026

Mother's Day is the perfect occasion to create something truly special with your hands. Embroidery allows you to add a personal touch that store-bought gifts just can't match. Here are 10 ideas to inspire your next project.

1. Personalised Tea Towels

Stitch a floral design with her initial or a sweet message. Use our Mother's Day embroidery designs for perfect results.

2. Hoop Art Wall Decor

Create a mini masterpiece in an embroidery hoop. “Home is where Mom is” or a simple flower bouquet make lovely gifts.

3. Personalised Towel Set

Add a monogram or a cute motif to a set of bath towels. Practical and sentimental.

Shop our favourite towel embroidery designs →

4. Memory Quilt Square

If you have a group of siblings, each can embroider a square with a memory, then sew them together.

5. Embroidered Pouch

A small zippered pouch with a floral motif – perfect for jewellery or cosmetics.

6. Mother & Child Portrait

Use an embroidery digitised design to create a silhouette of mother and child.

7. Kitchen Towel Set

Match a set of flour sack towels with seasonal motifs – flowers, bees, or “Mama’s Kitchen”.

8. Baby Bib with Grandma's Name

For new grandmas, a personalised bib is a thoughtful gift.

9. Embroidered Greeting Card

Stitch a small design onto cardstock and frame it as a card that lasts forever.

10. Tote Bag with “Best Mom Ever”

A reusable tote with a cute embroidery makes a stylish and useful gift.
